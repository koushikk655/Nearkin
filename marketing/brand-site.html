about Neario

Why we built
this.

An honest note about what we believe, who we built Neario for, and what we hope to break.

a small manifesto

Not another marketplace.

India's home makers and corner shops have existed for generations. The home baker. The kirana on your street. The pickle aunty whose orders happen on WhatsApp. The craft maker selling DM-to-DM on Instagram. The sweet shop that's been there forty years and still uses a paper notebook.

They sell to their locality. They know their customers by name. They deliver themselves. They've never needed a national marketplace — and frankly, a national marketplace has never needed them.

So we built Neario for them. A platform that puts the shops next door into your pocket — without asking them to change how they work.

We don't take a percentage. We don't run their delivery for them. We don't pressure them to discount. We charge a small flat fee per order for the infrastructure — phone OTP, payment processing, order inbox, push notifications. Sellers keep the rest. Specific pricing comes at launch.

This isn't an attempt to "disrupt" local commerce. It's an attempt to make local commerce work, as it always has, but with less friction.

what we believe

Three principles.

Locality first.

Discovery is geographic, not algorithmic. Buyers see shops within 5 km. Sellers reach buyers within 5 km. No national bidding, no surge pricing.

Real people.

Every shop is a real person — Anita, Priya, Karan. Their name is on the order. You can message them. They deliver themselves. No faceless catalog, no warehouse fleet.

Fair economics.

A small flat fee — never a percentage that grows with the price tag. Never surge pricing. Weekly UPI payouts straight to the seller's bank. What sellers earn, sellers keep. Specific pricing at launch.

the team

A small group, building in public.

Neario is a small founding team based in India. We're hiring as we grow — engineers, designers, seller ops, city leads.

questions and answers

FAQ.

Short answers to common questions. Split into things buyers ask and things sellers ask. Reach out at hello@neario.in for anything not covered here.

For buyers

How is Neario different from Zomato / Swiggy / Amazon?
Those are national marketplaces with their own delivery fleets. Neario only shows shops within 5 km of you, and the sellers deliver themselves. Every shop is a real person, not a catalog listing. You're buying from a neighbour, not a warehouse.
What if I'm not in a launch city yet?
Drop your phone number on the home page. We'll text you the install link the day we go live in your locality. One SMS, that's it. We don't run newsletters or send promo spam.
How do I pay?
UPI (via Razorpay) or cash on delivery. The seller gets to decide which they accept, and you'll see your options at checkout. Most sellers accept both.
Who delivers my order?
The seller — themselves. Most go by scooter. Some walk. Occasionally a teenage cousin gets dispatched. Because the delivery radius is only 5 km, this works.
What happens if a seller cancels?
You get a full refund within 24 hours if you paid via UPI. If you ordered with cash on delivery, there's nothing to refund — your order just doesn't happen. You'll get a push notification within minutes of the cancellation.
Can I message the seller before ordering?
Yes. Every shop page has a "Message shop" button. The seller usually replies within an hour during their business hours. Useful for custom orders, festival deliveries, etc.

For sellers

What will Neario charge?
We're pre-launch and haven't finalised pricing publicly. Our commitment: fair, transparent, and never a hidden percentage that grows with your sale price. Full pricing will be on this page the moment we open for sellers. Sign up to the waitlist and we'll share it with you first.
How will payouts work?
Weekly. Every Monday morning, the previous week's sales hit your UPI ID or bank account. No minimum threshold. No paperwork. We're targeting Monday-morning settlement from day one.
Do I need GST?
Only if your annual turnover crosses the GST threshold (currently ₹40 lakh for most goods, ₹20 lakh for some services). Below that, no GST mandatory. We'll help you register when you cross it.
Can I sell from home?
Yes — that's actually our core audience. Home bakers, tiffin services, craft makers, pickle / preserve sellers, anyone making something at home and selling locally. Neario doesn't require commercial premises.
What if I run a physical shop already?
Even better. Sweet shops, kiranas, ateliers, boutiques, small restaurants — Neario gives you a digital storefront without forcing you onto a website. You just open the seller app each morning.
Who delivers?
You do. Within your 5 km delivery radius (you set it). We don't run a delivery fleet — that's what keeps our fees flat and predictable.
What if I close my shop temporarily?
Flip the OPEN/CLOSED toggle in the seller app. While closed, your shop doesn't appear in buyer feeds, and no new orders come in. Flip it back on whenever you're ready.
