What it is

Neario makes the independent businesses around you discoverable — and lets you buy, maker to doorstep.

✕

Not quick commerce

No dark stores, no ten-minute promises, no warehouse full of the same forty SKUs.

✕

Not another marketplace

No faceless wall of resellers racing to the cheapest price. Real people, real kitchens, real shelves.

✕

Not a social feed

No doomscroll. Every screen exists for one reason — to help you find something good, close by.

Every locality is quietly full of brilliant little businesses. The aunty whose pickles your whole lane swears by. The home baker taking orders over a stretched-thin WhatsApp. The potter two turns away you've simply never found.

They don't need a warehouse or a delivery fleet. They need to be found — by the people already living minutes from their door. That's the whole idea. Neario is the layer that puts your locality's makers on the map, and lets you buy from them directly.
