Good questions

Things people ask us.

When is Neario launching? +
Soon — locality by locality, so every area launches with real sellers already on board. Early-access members get in first when their area goes live.
Which cities are you starting with? +
We're deciding launch areas based on where sellers and buyers sign up — so the honest answer is: wherever the waitlist is loudest. Join it, and bring your locality with you.
How is this different from Swiggy, Zepto or Amazon? +
Those platforms move inventory through warehouses and fleets. Neario doesn't hold stock, run delivery, or sell anything itself — it connects you directly to independent sellers near you, who deliver themselves. Discovery, not logistics.
I sell from home. What do I need to join? +
Just what you already have — your products, your phone, and your locality. Join the seller waitlist and we'll walk you through setting up your storefront when your area opens.
